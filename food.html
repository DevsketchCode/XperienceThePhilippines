Food

The Philippines is covered with delicious fresh fruits and vegetables, from bananas and coconuts right from the treetops to the best tasting mangos you've ever had. There is also an abundance of rice from the many rice fields across the country to enjoy with any savory dish. Then let's not forget the fresh seafood that is readily available that was just caught moments before. Hover over an item to see a larger image and a description.
