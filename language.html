Language

Although there are over 120 individual languages or dialects in the Philippines, the majority of the people know either Tagalog or Cebuano and English is actually their second language. Below is a list of some common Tagalog phrases that can help you get started.

Review Common Tagalog Words, and try out the quiz!

English Tagalog Phonetics
