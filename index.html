Attractions

The Philippines is an amazing country that consists of over 7,100 islands in southeastern Asia. The Philippine islands offer breathtaking sceneries that capture your heart. If shopping is more of your thing, then the Philippines is also the home of some of the largest shopping malls in the world. Visit the Attractions

Transportation

You'll find the usual personal vehicles, buses, and taxis in the Philippines. However there are a few forms of transportation that are more exclusive to the Philippines. Show the Transportation

Photographs of different Filipino food

Food

With a large amount of fresh food available within the country, you'll find some of the best tasting meals that you've ever had. The main fresh foods that are widely available are seafood, fruits, vegetables, and most definitely rice. Show the Food

Picture of different language translations

Language

There are over 120 individual languages in the Philippines. As you travel to the Philippines, you'll find that the majority of the people know Tagalog or Cebuano and English is actually their second language. Learn Some Tagalog
